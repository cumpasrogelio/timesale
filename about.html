Time Sale watches was founded in 2016 by Rogelio Cumpas, with the mission to sell serious timepieces with quality, durability and eye-catching iconic designs in mind. Each Padron Watch is hand made with 316L surgical-grade stainless steel and sapphire crystal, using only the highest grade watch movements.

Located in Baguio City,Philippines each Time Sale watches is originally designed and engineered, using no common components apart from the movements. They are hand built, individually regulated for accurate timekeeping, and then packed and shipped from here.
